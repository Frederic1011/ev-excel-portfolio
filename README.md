# EV Market Analysis (Excel)
This project presents an Excel-based market analysis of the global electric vehicle (EV) industry using IEA data.

## Objective
- Analyze global EV sales growth over time
- Identify regional contributions to growth
- Examine powertrain mix shifts (BEV vs PHEV)

## Methodology
- Data source: IEA Global EV Data 2024
- Tools: Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables & Charts)
- Scope: Historical EV sales for passenger cars (2010â€“2023)

## Key Insights
- Global EV sales grew rapidly (~74% CAGR from 2010 to 2023), with strong acceleration after 2020
- China is the primary driver of global EV sales growth, followed by Europe and North America
- BEVs have become the dominant contributor to EV sales growth over time
